About Me

It all started for Mika over a decade ago when she was struggling with her own acne problems. Desperate to find a solution she made the decision to attend school for skin care and became a licensed esthetician and now has been practicing for the past fifteen years. It wasn’t until she started learning how to heal her bad skin by working from the inside-out, optimizing her digestion and in the process creating a unique philosophy that would ultimately become her healthy living lifestyle.

After a few years Mika developed a passion for fitness and how it correlates with nutrition, beauty, & overall wellness and began revitalizing her routine. In 2011 Mika started blogging her transformation and sharing her healthy lifestyle on social media. Little did she know how much of an inspiration she was to other people on the same journey, as a results she received amazing feedback! Mika noticed she got more people coming to her with a lot of different questions from losing weight, building muscle, improving their eating habits, or just even improving the appearance of their skin. That’s when she decided to create this platform to help coach people to achieve their needs. She furthered her education on nutrition and fitness, falling in love more and more with this lifestyle. Mika reveals that she personally never felt or looked better, and her skin is healthier than it's ever been!

Mika’s approach is unique: She believes living a better and more fulfilled life, and that happiness starts within the body… an overall healthy lifestyle. She has spent the last 5 plus years truly embracing this philosophy. It is her mission now to educate those around her on how they can achieve their goals. Whether it’s in the form of food, love, or experiences, the choice of what we use to fuel ourselves with will affect who we are.

Mika uses her knowledge approaching nutrition, fitness, and empowerment to help you live in balance, harmony, and happiness with your true self. Her guidance is based on scientific education and personal experience. Fusing the science of nutrition with fitness my goal is to help you to make better health and food decisions offering health and fitness coaching through a personalized approach.

Mika now designs meal plans, training/workout programs, recipes and tips that work for everyone regardless of their lifestyle. Teaching others to discover their ultimate health and beauty by following her principles and without having to obsess or go through extreme dieting programs. She is dedicated to providing you with all the tools you need to achieve this through nutrition, fitness, empowerment, beauty tips and more. Her goal is to help you live your healthiest and most beautiful life!
